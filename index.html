We support Visa, MasterCard, Amex, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, UPI, net banking, digital wallets, and 20+ cryptocurrencies.

How long does integration take?

Most businesses go live within 1 hour using our drop-in checkout or SDKs. Enterprise integrations typically take 1-2 weeks.

Are there setup or hidden fees?

Zero setup fees, zero monthly minimums on Starter plan. You only pay a small percentage per successful transaction.
