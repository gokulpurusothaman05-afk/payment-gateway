Our Story

Building the Payment Infrastructure of Tomorrow

Founded in 2014, Stackly emerged from a vision to democratize secure payment processing for businesses of every size. What began as a small fintech startup has evolved into a global platform processing over $12 billion annually.

We believe payments should be invisible, instant, and secure — empowering merchants to focus on what they do best while we handle the complexity behind every transaction.
