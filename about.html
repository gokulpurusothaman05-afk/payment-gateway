About Stackly

Leading the payment revolution since 2014

Our Story

Founded in 2014, Stackly was born from a simple idea: make payments simple, secure, and accessible for everyone. What started as a small team of passionate developers has grown into a global payment solutions provider serving over 50,000 businesses worldwide.

We believe in the power of technology to transform how money moves around the world, and we're committed to building the most reliable, secure, and innovative payment infrastructure.

Our Mission

To empower businesses of all sizes with secure, reliable, and innovative payment solutions that drive growth and success.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted payment platform, enabling seamless transactions across all channels and geographies.
